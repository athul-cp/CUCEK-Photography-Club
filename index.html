About Us

CUCEK Photography Club from CUSAT

Photography is one of the best ways to express ourselves, capture our special moments, inspire our imaginations and to see things others won't notice. We CUCEK photography club is here to provide these opportunities to those who are passionate in photography. Our community aims at gaining valuable knowledge by connecting various photography enthusiasts to share their images and perspectives

We aim at coordinating people for various upcoming Photography competitions.Our goal is to help all members become the photographers they wish to be. The Club conducts monthly General Meetings and Scheduled Special Interest Group sessions, Workshops, Classes and Field Trips to help members learn about photography and enjoy the process.
