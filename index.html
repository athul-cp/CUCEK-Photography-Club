About Us

CUCEK Photography Club from CUSAT

We aim at growing and improving the creative community .From 2018 onwards CUCEK Photography Club (CPC) is a part of CUSAT.

Our goal is to help all members become the photographers they wish to be. The Club conducts monthly General Meetings and Scheduled Special Interest Group sessions, Workshops, Classes and Field Trips to help members learn about photography and enjoy the process.
